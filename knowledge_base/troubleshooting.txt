# Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Connection Problems

If you're experiencing connection issues with our service:

1. **Check your internet connection**: Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
2. **Clear browser cache**: Sometimes cached data can cause connection issues. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
3. **Try a different browser**: Some features may work better in certain browsers.
4. **Disable VPN or proxy**: If you're using a VPN or proxy, try disabling it temporarily.
5. **Check firewall settings**: Your firewall might be blocking our service. Add our domain to the allowlist.

### Login Issues

If you can't log in to your account:

1. **Reset password**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
2. **Check email address**: Make sure you're using the correct email address associated with your account.
3. **Check for typos**: Ensure there are no typos in your email or password.
4. **Check caps lock**: Make sure caps lock is not enabled when typing your password.
5. **Try incognito mode**: Browser extensions might interfere with the login process.

### Performance Issues

If our service is running slowly:

1. **Check your internet speed**: Slow internet can affect performance.
2. **Close unnecessary applications**: Other applications might be using resources.
3. **Update your browser**: Outdated browsers may not perform optimally.
4. **Disable browser extensions**: Some extensions can slow down web applications.
5. **Clear local storage**: Try clearing your browser's local storage.

### Mobile App Issues

If you're having problems with our mobile app:

1. **Update the app**: Make sure you have the latest version of our app.
2. **Restart the app**: Close the app completely and reopen it.
3. **Restart your device**: Sometimes a simple device restart can fix issues.
4. **Check device storage**: Insufficient storage can cause app malfunctions.
5. **Reinstall the app**: As a last resort, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

## Error Codes

Here are some common error codes you might encounter and what they mean:

- **Error 404**: Page not found. The requested page does not exist.
- **Error 500**: Internal server error. There's a problem on our end.
- **Error 403**: Forbidden. You don't have permission to access the requested resource.
- **Error 429**: Too many requests. You've exceeded the rate limit.
- **Error 503**: Service unavailable. The service is temporarily down for maintenance.

If you encounter an error not listed here, please contact our support team with the error code and a description of what you were doing when the error occurred.
